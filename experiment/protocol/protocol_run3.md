# Simulation Protocol Results

**Problem:** How do we build a retirement system for people who don't know how much they will earn next month, where "consistency" is impossible?

**Context:** Traditional retirement systems (401k, pensions, IRAs) assume regular income and consistent contributions. This excludes ~36% of the US workforce engaged in gig work, freelancing, seasonal employment, or informal economy.

**Seeds:** palouser, critique, bromobenzyl, gnomically, remilitarize

**Date:** 2025-12-08

---

## World 1: PALOUSER

### World Rules
A world where nothing moves in straight lines. All matter, energy, and time flows in rolling waves. Strength comes from layered accumulation over cycles—a wall built in a day is tissue-paper weak; one layered over years is unbreakable. Resources naturally flow to "troughs" (low points). Everything operates in growth-dormancy cycles; pushing through dormancy damages things.

### Solution: The Sediment Basin System
- **Personal Troughs**: Every worker has a Basin where value naturally flows. Good months = excess rolls downhill into Basin automatically
- **Dust-Catching Architecture**: Every transaction releases micro-particles (e.g., purchase of $7.30 releases $0.70 as "dust" into Basin)
- **Valley Collectives**: Workers in same trade form shared troughs—collective sits lower, attracts more flow
- **Cyclical Protection**: Dormant members (no income) stay in collective while others layer; no penalty for natural cycles
- **Layer-Count Measurement**: Retirement readiness = number of contribution cycles, not total amount

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Frequency Over Magnitude—consistent small contributions over many cycles create more resilient outcomes than large sporadic contributions.

### Bridge to Reality
| Fictional Element | Real Implementation |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Dust-catching | Transaction round-ups + micro-percentage skims (1-3% of every payment) |
| Valley Collectives | Occupation-based pooled funds with portable membership |
| Cyclical Protection | Contribution holidays without penalty; maintain status during zero-income months |
| Layer-count | Contribution frequency score as primary metric for benefits/matching |
| Natural erosion | Variable withdrawal rates tied to needs, not fixed percentages |

## World 2: CRITIQUE

### World Rules
A world where nothing can persist without being actively examined. Matter gains density proportional to critique rigor. Anything unexamined for ~7 days fades and eventually ceases to exist. To critique something, you must expose an equivalent aspect of yourself. Love is demonstrated through caring critique.

### Solution: The Critique-Pool Retirement System
- **Contribution Fluidity**: Contribute whatever you can; peers examine ("Is this genuine surplus? Sustainable for you?"). Critique solidifies contribution
- **Symmetric Exposure Bonds**: Joining means exposing your income patterns; they expose theirs. Creates mutual visibility, trust through shared vulnerability
- **Sustainer Rotation**: Members take turns examining Pool's wealth. Earning well = sustain more. Low income = others cover
- **Fallow Period Protocol**: Zero-income months = contribute critique labor instead. Maintain membership through attention, not money
- **Living Retirement**: Retirees remain active in Pools; their experience makes them valuable Sustainers

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Mutual Accountability as Wealth Preservation—financial commitments gain durability through peer verification; participation in verification creates reciprocal obligation.

### Bridge to Reality
| Fictional Element | Real Implementation |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Critique Pools | Small pools (8-15) with quarterly contribution reviews |
| Symmetric Exposure | Members share income documentation; mutual visibility |
| Sustainer Rotation | Rotating stewardship duties |
| Fallow Protocol | Contribute stewardship hours during low-income periods; hours convert to credits |
| Living Retirement | Retirees stay in pool, contribute as mentors/institutional memory |

**Starting Point:** Form pilot with 10 people who already trust each other. Shared spreadsheet for transparency, monthly video calls for review, simple shared bank account.

## World 3: BROMOBENZYL

### World Rules
A world where nothing exists in stable, final state. Everything is a "reactive intermediate" seeking transformation through bonding. Isolation causes physical decay. Each bond transforms both parties. Connections require intentional "activation energy." Every commitment releases something (the "leaving group"). Unbonded entities become dangerously unstable.

### Solution: The Transformation Pool System
- **Catalyst Contribution Model**: Fresh income is unstable—bond it immediately to Pool. Any amount works; Pool needs contributions to remain stable (mutual need)
- **Activation Energy Advantage**: Each income event = "bonding pulse." 12 small contributions generate MORE transformation energy than 2 large ones. Irregular workers have advantage
- **Leaving Group Dividend**: Every contribution releases a "Departure Token" (what you gave up). Tokens accumulate and remain yours—become your withdrawal mechanism at retirement
- **Stabilizing Ligands**: Zero income? Pool sends small stabilizing payments preventing decay. No one becomes dangerously disconnected
- **Catalytic Inversion**: At retirement, you don't exit—you become stabilizing element for younger workers

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Frequency-Based Contribution with Behavioral Byproduct Capture—many small intentional acts create more psychological ownership than fewer large acts; make opportunity cost visible as an asset.

### Bridge to Reality
| Fictional Element | Real Implementation |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Micro-bonding | Every income event triggers contribution prompt (3% auto with one-tap confirm) |
| Activation advantage | Frequency Multiplier: 53+ contributions/year = 1.10x bonus credits |
| Departure Tokens | "Flex Credit Ledger"—visual dashboard showing deferred purchasing power |
| Stabilizing ligands | Stability Reserve (0.5% of contributions) funds micro-deposits during 3+ month gaps |
| Catalytic Inversion | "Pool Mentor" role: reduced fees in exchange for engagement |

**Key Innovation:** Reframe gig workers' irregular income as MORE activation events rather than less stability.

## World 4: GNOMICALLY

### World Rules
A world where density of meaning is literal power. The more truth compressed into fewer words, the more energy released. Verbose structures crumble. Currency is stamped aphorisms. Contracts are single sentences. Paradoxes containing truth are load-bearing. Meaningful silence has physical weight.

### Solution: Ratio-Anchored Vessels (The Tithe-Seed)
- **The Seed**: Each worker carries token inscribed with RATIO, not amount. "One-tenth of what passes through me condenses here." Doesn't care if you earned 3 or 3,000
- **Automatic Sublimation**: When income arrives, appropriate portion transfers automatically. No decision. No willpower
- **Paradox Stability**: "I save the same amount by saving different amounts." Ratio constant, absolute varies. Consistency through inconsistency
- **Silence Weight**: Zero-income months with genuine hardship count as "potential-density"—structural integrity, not failure
- **Communal Pools**: Individual Seeds merge for mutual compensation. Self-referential truth generates balancing power
- **Proportional Bloom**: At retirement, Seed expands proportionally to how it was built

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Ratio-Based Automation with Collective Buffering—remove the decision point by anchoring to proportion rather than amount; smooth volatility through pooling.

### Bridge to Reality
| Fictional Element | Real Implementation |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Tithe-Seed ratio | User-defined percentage (10-15%) from each deposit, auto-transferred |
| Automatic sublimation | Income-triggered automation via Qapital, Digit, or bank percentage transfers |
| Silence weight | Pre-defined "legitimate zero" rules—when zero is acceptable without guilt |
| Communal Pools | Cohort-based pooling with liquidity lending from collective surplus |
| Proportional bloom | Variable withdrawal tied to contribution pattern |

**Policy Recommendation:** Gig platforms build ratio-based retirement withholding into payment flows. Tax-advantaged "variable income retirement accounts" with percentage-based limits instead of dollar limits.

## World 5: REMILITARIZE

### World Rules
A world where everything must continuously re-arm and re-fortify to maintain existence. Static defenses lose 50% capacity every ~30-day cycle. Life follows mobilization calendars (3-5 day periods monthly). Collective defense is 3x more efficient than individual. Defensive capacity is visible to all as "readiness aura."

### Solution: Cyclical Contribution Pools with Readiness-Based Accounting
- **Mobilization Cycles**: During each monthly period, contribute whatever surplus you can. Amount doesn't matter—participation matters. Each contribution = "mobilization credit"
- **Readiness Aura Tracking**: Track cumulative participation, not balances. "Contributed during 200 cycles over working life" matters more than total amount
- **Coalition Defense at Retirement**: Retirees stop maintaining personal defense. Convert to coalition coverage—group defends them. 3x more efficient
- **Flexible Entry Points**: Feast = more credits. Famine = less. Zero income = "service mobilization" (labor instead of resources)
- **Intergenerational Pacts**: Working-age defend retired; in exchange, their future standing secured

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Participation-History Accounting vs. Balance-Sheet Accounting—track consistent participation in mutual-aid cycles rather than isolated accumulation. Behavior patterns matter more than outcomes.

### Bridge to Reality
| Fictional Element | Real Implementation |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Mobilization cycles | Monthly/quarterly participation periods |
| Readiness tracking | Credit system: cycle count (primary) + cumulative contribution (secondary) |
| Flexible contribution | Cash, service hours (eldercare, community work), skill contribution—all convert to credits |
| Visible aura | Public participation ledger (blockchain); members see each other's cycle counts |
| Coalition retirement | Benefit calculation: base tied to participation count, modifier for cumulative |

**Pilot:** Professional guild or industry association. Freelancers contribute monthly (any amount); service contributions count; 15 years participation = retirement benefits scaled to cycle count.

## Synthesis: Most Promising Mechanisms

**All five solutions converged on common principles:**

### 1. Measure Frequency, Not Magnitude
Every world independently arrived at: counting contribution events matters more than counting dollars. This suggests a deep truth: behavioral consistency predicts retirement success better than balance snapshots.

### 2. Automatic Percentage-Based Capture
The "Tithe-Seed" (gnomically) is the cleanest expression: set a ratio once, let it scale automatically. No repeated decisions. No willpower tax. Adapts to income variability by design.

### 3. Collective Buffering
All solutions included pooling mechanisms where individual volatility cancels out at group level. The insight: gig workers facing uncorrelated income variance can stabilize each other.

### 4. Non-Monetary Contribution During Gaps
Critique and Remilitarize both allowed labor/attention as valid contribution during zero-income periods. This prevents psychological exit from the system during hardship.

### 5. Role Continuity, Not Exit
Three worlds (critique, bromobenzyl, remilitarize) had retirees shift roles rather than withdraw. Elders become stabilizers, mentors, sustainers—creating continuity and fighting isolation.

## Recommended Implementation Priority

**Tier 1: Immediately Implementable**
- Percentage-based auto-transfers (already supported by most banks/apps)
- Transaction round-ups directed to retirement savings
- Contribution frequency tracking as dashboard metric

**Tier 2: Product Innovation Needed**
- Gig platform integration (Uber, DoorDash, Upwork) with ratio-based withholding
- Frequency Multiplier credits rewarding contribution events
- Stability Reserve funded by collective to cover gap periods
- Flex Credit Ledger visualizing deferred purchasing power

**Tier 3: Policy/Institutional Change**
- Tax-advantaged "Variable Income Retirement Accounts" with percentage-based (not dollar-based) limits
- Incentives for contribution frequency, not just contribution amount
- Service hours as valid retirement contribution (time-banking integration)

---

## The Core Insight

Traditional retirement systems fail gig workers by demanding what they cannot provide: consistency of amount.

These five impossible worlds all discovered the same escape: **consistency of proportion** or **consistency of participation** can substitute for consistency of amount—and may actually produce more resilient outcomes.

The gig worker contributing 10% of highly variable income across 200+ months builds stronger retirement security than the salaried worker making 20 large annual deposits. The system just needs to be designed to recognize that.
