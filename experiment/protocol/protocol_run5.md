# Simulation Protocol Results

**Problem:** How do we build a retirement system for people who don't know how much they will earn next month? Traditional retirement systems (401k, pensions, IRAs) assume regular income and consistent contributions. This excludes ~36% of the US workforce engaged in gig work, freelancing, seasonal employment, or informal economy.

**Seeds:** phraseman, desperacy, pidan, phosis, theca

**Model:** Claude Opus 4.5 (5 parallel agents per phase)

---

## World 1: PHRASEMAN (Linguistic Materialism)

### World Rules
In this world, phrases are the fundamental particles of reality. Matter, energy, and space are woven from spoken/written word combinations. Phrases have mass proportional to specificity and emotional resonance. All phrases decay unless maintained through repetition by Chorus Guilds. Contradictory phrases annihilate each other. Phrases ARE currency - the wealthy hoard them; the poor must spend immediately. Buildings are spoken into existence. Death comes when no one speaks your name anymore.

### Solution: The Perpetual Chorus (Phrase-Pool Retirement System)
- **Resonance Pools**: Collective phrase-reservoirs where irregular earners deposit whatever they can, whenever they can. The Pool exists as an echoing chamber where phrases maintain themselves through acoustics rather than individual repetition.
- **Echo-Rights**: Deposits earn the right to have the Pool speak on your behalf later. Rights accumulate based on weight/quality and duration reverberating.
- **Compound Effect**: Phrases echo off each other, creating clusters more durable than individuals could maintain alone.
- **Proportional Benefits**: Allocation based on total accumulated Echo-Rights, not any single contribution.
- **Mutual Speaking Covenant**: Other members' stronger phrases shore up the Pool's integrity when yours decay.
- **Early Echo**: Emergency advances borrowing against future Echo-Rights.

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Pooled Resilience Through Collective Buffering - irregular, unpredictable individual contributions can be smoothed into reliable collective outputs when aggregated across many participants with uncorrelated timing patterns.

**Bridge to Reality:**
Create a "Flex-Pool Retirement Cooperative" where:
- Members contribute any amount, any time (no minimum, no schedule)
- Contributions timestamped as "contribution-units" that compound over time
- Benefits proportional to lifetime accumulated units, not current balance
- Emergency advances structured as borrowing from future self
- Mutual covenant provides floor guarantee through pooled reserves
- App-based contributions (round-ups, percentage of deposits, manual)

## World 2: DESPERACY (Need-Gravity)

### World Rules
Desperate need is a fundamental force (Need-Pull) that bends space, attracts resources, and warps probability. Only genuine need generates pull; false need creates repulsive "Hollow Push." High need contracts distances to solutions. Resources naturally migrate toward highest Need-Pull. Once need is met, pull drops (4hr half-life) - the well-fed cannot easily acquire more food. The wealthy must maintain artificial mild needs to prevent resources hemorrhaging away.

### Solution: Temporal Need Anchoring System (TNAS)
- **Future-Self Need Bonding**: Legal connection between current self and future desperate retired self. Future need creates pull on current resources.
- **Need Projection Certification**: At age 25-30, certify likely future need trajectory as an anchor.
- **Satiation Delay Chambers**: 10,000 workers pool Future-Self Bonds. Combined future-need creates massive stable pull.
- **Need-Differential Harvesting**: When you earn more than you need, you repel resources. Harvesters capture what's drifting away and channel it to your Future-Self Bond. Physics decides contribution, not you.
- **Mild-Need Maintenance Protocol**: Retirees receive resources in need-synchronized pulses - never desperate, never fully satiated.
- **Hollow-Push Fraud Prevention**: False claims generate repulsion, making fraud self-defeating.

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Temporal Self-Binding + Need-Based Automatic Allocation - treating your future self as a separate stakeholder with legitimate claims, and removing voluntary decision-making from contribution amounts based on objective surplus.

**Bridge to Reality:**
- "Future Needs Certificate" at age 25-30 becomes a legal beneficiary of current earnings
- Surplus-Based Auto-Contribution: rates adjust based on income-to-essential-expenses ratio
- Pooled Future-Claim Funds with individual tracking
- Buffer-Threshold Distribution: resources release when liquid buffer drops below 2-month threshold
- Key insight: **Reduce decision-points where present-self can override future-self's interests**

## World 3: PIDAN (Preservation Through Transformation)

### World Rules
Stasis is entropy. Anything unchanged decays and dissolves. The only way to preserve something is to continuously transform it. Surface changes buy little time; true preservation requires fundamental transformation. Currency evaporates if hoarded. Careers don't exist - people practice "transformation crafts." Relationships cycle through phases. Memories must be retold to survive.

### Solution: Transformation Accumulation System (TAS)
- **Transformation Ledger**: Save documented records of changes undergone, not money.
- **Gig Worker Advantage Paradox**: Stable career = almost no transformation credit. Variable earners are "transformation-rich" - every gig, shift, hustle is banked.
- **Variable Contribution Recognition**:
  - High income: Convert excess into transformation bonds (fund others' education, career changes)
  - Low income: You're transforming intensely - this period IS your contribution
  - Micro-transformations: Retelling memories, helping others transform
- **Retirement Threshold = Transformation Saturation**: Not age-based but completion-percentage based.
- **Memory Bank**: Memory Guilds retell your life story. Creates transformation inheritance.
- **Relational Transformation**: Constantly shifting client relationships are bankable assets.

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Reframe Instability as Accumulated Capital - proof of navigation through change is itself a form of wealth that can be banked, transferred, and redeemed.

**Bridge to Reality:**
- **Portable Transformation Credentials**: Verified "experience passport" documenting industries worked, skills demonstrated, client diversity
- **Contribution Recognition Restructuring**: "Low income but high transformation" periods receive contribution credits
- **Transformation Bonds**: High earners invest in others' transitions with returns tied to success
- **Relational Asset Recognition**: Platforms track "relationship capital" - repeat clients, referral networks
- **Mentorship Economy**: Experienced gig workers paid to document navigation strategies

## World 4: PHOSIS (Illumination as Existence)

### World Rules
Observation × Illumination = Existence. Nothing has inherent permanence - existence depends on focused light and attention received. Objects gain "existential mass" through sustained illumination. Unobserved things dissolve into probability. Multiple observers multiply (not add) their effect. Attention is primary currency. Professional Observers are paid to maintain artifacts, stabilize patients. The Fading are isolated people becoming translucent at edges.

### Solution: Collective Illumination Retirement System (CIRS)
- **Observation Circles**: Groups of 50-200 irregular earners who collectively "illuminate" each other's retirements.
- **Multiplication Effect**: Multiple observers multiply effect. Small irregular contributions from many create more stability than large regular from one.
- **Light-When-Able Protocol**: Bright periods = contribute more; Dim periods = contribute nothing but remain observed. Total observer-hours matter, not consistency.
- **Watcher's Dividend**: Retirees become "Witnesses" for active members, creating virtuous cycle.
- **Anti-Fading Protocols**: Presence Circles (regular gatherings), Emergency Illumination Teams, Memory Mirror Fund.
- **Micro-Illumination Deposits**: Round-ups, seasonal surges, barter-attention conversion.

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Mutual Accountability Through Witnessed Commitment - commitment becomes more binding when witnessed by others who share your stake. Social visibility creates behavioral persistence.

**Bridge to Reality:**
- **Retirement Circles**: 20-50 people with monthly video calls + quarterly in-person gatherings
- Legal wrapper as 501(c)(12) benevolent association or cooperative
- Sliding scale contributions (0-15%) with no penalty for $0 months
- Retirees remain stakeholders (attend meetings, mentor, vote, receive wisdom stipend)
- Commitment ceremonies and peer accountability partners
- Hardship fund (10% of assets) with peer support network
- Essentially: **rediscovering mutual aid societies with modern tools**

## World 5: THECA (Containment as Existence)

### World Rules
Nothing exists without a container. Existence = Content + Container. A container can only hold what its nature permits. Moving between containers always costs something (transfer friction). Containers within containers amplify containment. Every container has selective permeability. Social class = sophistication of nested containers. Learning = crafting internal containers.

### Solution: Nested Flow-Vessel System
- **Flux Catch-Basin**: Container whose aperture automatically adjusts to income flow. Heavy = widens; thin = narrows. Only captures overflow after essential needs met.
- **Micro-Containment Array**:
  - Windfall Vessels: Deep, narrow - activated only when income >150% of rolling average
  - Steady-Drip Vessels: Shallow, wide - capture small amounts from every transaction
  - Seasonal Vessels: Time-locked, seal during known low-income periods
  - Emergency Bypass Vessels: One-way valves, release only under crisis
- **Community Bridging-Tank**: Collective container pooling contributions. Reduces friction, merges chaotic flows.
- **Permeable Time-Membrane**: Viewing access, crisis permeability, milestone gates, prevents complete drainage.
- **Contribution-Echo System**: Thin capture-membrane skims micro-percentage from every transaction.
- **Five-Layer Nesting**: National Trust → Regional Tanks → Personal Portfolios → Flux Catch-Basin → Micro-Vessel Array

### Extracted Mechanism
**Label:** PORTABLE

**Core Principle:** Adaptive Segmentation with Behavioral Friction Architecture - partition savings into purpose-specific accounts with varying accessibility rules, automatic flow-based contribution rates, and graduated withdrawal barriers.

**Bridge to Reality:**
**"FlowSave" Product Design:**
- Income Detection Layer via Plaid/bank API
- Adaptive Contribution Engine:
  - Base rate: 3% after bills paid
  - Windfall trigger: 15% of deposits >150% of 90-day average
  - Drought mode: 0% when below 2-week buffer
- Bucket Structure: Emergency, Retirement (IRA), Seasonal Buffer, Opportunity Fund
- Micro-Capture: Round-ups + 0.5% skim on deposits over $500
- 72-hour cooling period for withdrawals
- Key insight: **Savings architecture matters more than savings willpower**

## Most Promising Mechanisms

### Tier 1: Immediately Implementable

1. **Adaptive Flow-Based Contributions (theca)** - The most technically straightforward. Fintech already has pieces (round-ups, variable transfers). Needs integration into coherent system for variable earners. Build "FlowSave" as described.

2. **Retirement Circles / Witnessed Commitment (phosis)** - Already exists as ROSCAs globally. Needs modernization with app layer, explicit variable-contribution tolerance, and regulatory pathway. Partner with gig platforms or freelancer unions.

### Tier 2: Policy Innovation Required

3. **Transformation Credentials (pidan)** - Requires building verification infrastructure. Partner with gig platforms to create "experience passports." Advocate for crediting high-transformation/low-income periods toward retirement.

4. **Future-Self Legal Standing (desperacy)** - Most radical but highest potential impact. Would require new legal/regulatory frameworks. Could pilot as enhanced commitment devices with binding arbitration.

### Tier 3: Systemic Redesign

5. **Flex-Pool Cooperatives (phraseman)** - Requires cooperative formation, actuarial modeling, and regulatory approval. Longer timeline but addresses root issue: collective buffering for irregular earners.

## Synthesis: The Unified Vision

All five worlds converge on a single insight: **Traditional retirement systems fail gig workers not because of individual failure, but because of architectural mismatch.**

The fictional physics made visible what's true but invisible in our world:
- Money decays faster for the poor (phraseman → forced immediate spending)
- Future needs have no claim on present resources (desperacy → temporal disconnect)
- Instability is punished rather than recognized (pidan → misvalued adaptability)
- Isolated savers dissolve (phosis → lack of social reinforcement)
- One-size containers don't fit variable flows (theca → architectural rigidity)

**The meta-solution:** Build retirement infrastructure that assumes income irregularity as the baseline, not the exception. This means:
1. Variable contribution rates tied to flow, not fixed schedules
2. Collective buffering across many irregular earners
3. Social witnessing for commitment persistence
4. Recognition of non-monetary contributions (transformation, mentorship, caregiving)
5. Graduated access barriers that prevent complete drainage while maintaining psychological ownership

The 36% of workers in irregular employment aren't failing to save—they're being failed by systems designed for a workforce that no longer exists.
